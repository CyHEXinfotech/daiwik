Manufacturing Excellence

Since its establishment in 2017, Daiwik Pharmasphere has been a pioneer in manufacturing top-quality pharmaceutical formulations.

We offer customized contract manufacturing services for Ointments, Cream, Gels and lotions. High quality standard combined with professional management make us a natural choice for pharmaceutical companies looking for contract manufacturing.

We distribute our products through multiple channels in Export market through merchant exporter and Domestic Market by reputed companies.

All our products are crafted in cGMP certified facilities, adhering strictly to WHO guidelines. Our dedicated team of experts manages every aspect from Research & Development to Production, Marketing, and distribution to ensure that our products meet global standards and customer specifications.

At Daiwik Pharmasphere, we understand the diverse requirements of different regions. We offer tailored packaging solutions designed to meet the specific needs of each market. Additionally, we provide private labeling services, offering our products under various brand identities to cater to the unique demands of our global clientele.
