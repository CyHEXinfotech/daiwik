Our Story

Daiwik Pharmasphere was established in 1975 as a trader and importer of API with a small unit of repacking of pharmaceutical products for human and veterinary use. Up till late ‘90s, Daiwik Pharmasphere was a strong supplier of veterinary medicines to various government hospitals in India. The Company diversified in late ‘90s with a focus on production of quality medicines. Today, Daiwik Pharmasphere has acquired a leading position in manufacturing and supplying high-quality Pharmaceutical formulations across the globe.

In the 40 years since its inception, the company excelled in manufacturing a complete range of products, viz. Tablets, Capsules, Syrups, Ointments, Dry Powder Injections, Liquid Injections, Ophthalmic solutions and Suppositories for multiple therapeutic areas. Daiwik Pharmasphere has also created a niche with its offerings of specialty products like Lozenges, Dermaceuticals, Aerosols, Mosquito Repellents, and Condoms.

The products are manufactured at cGMP certified facilities as per WHO guidelines, adhering to global production standards, with exports starting from 1994. Founded with core values of Commitment, Quality, and Service, Daiwik Pharmasphere has earned a great reputation in the International market. A number of customers have been with the company from the first export shipment. Such goodwill and enduring relationships have contributed enormously to Apex’s expansion and growth. Daiwik Pharmasphere is also a reliable supplier to a US-based NGO and a part of their country program around the world.

The company stays on the top with an impeccable combination of visionary leadership, excellent professionals, and state-of-the-art technology. “Quality medicines at affordable rates” is a motto that drives the team to excel and expand its reach to people across the world, regardless of geographic and socio-economic barriers. Vividly spread supply-chain capabilities across the world ensure the accessibility of the products anytime and every time. Apex’s endeavours include its contributions towards its Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives intentioned to make the world a better place to live in.

Manufacturing Excellence

Since its establishment in 1975, Daiwik Pharmasphere has been a pioneer in manufacturing top-quality pharmaceutical formulations. With state-of-the-art facilities located across India, we specialize in producing a wide range of products including Tablets, Capsules, Syrups, Ointments, Dry Powder Injections, Liquid Injections, Ophthalmic solutions, Suppositories, and specialty items such as Lozenges, Dermaceuticals, Aerosols, Mosquito Repellents, and Condoms.

All our products are crafted in cGMP certified facilities, adhering strictly to WHO guidelines. Our dedicated team of experts manages every aspect from Research & Development to Production, Marketing, and distribution to ensure that our products meet global standards and customer specifications.

At Daiwik Pharmasphere, we understand the diverse requirements of different regions. We offer tailored packaging solutions designed to meet the specific needs of each market. Additionally, we provide private labeling services, offering our products under various brand identities to cater to the unique demands of our global clientele.
