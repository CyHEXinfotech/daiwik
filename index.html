Manufacturing

Daiwik Pharmasphere excels in manufacturing world-class formulations in independent manufacturing facilities across India. Our facility is well-equipped with state-of-the-art machines and infrastructure, dedicated solely for the manufacturing of external preparations like creams, gels, ointments and lotions.

Our products are manufactured at cGMP certified facilities as per WHO guidelines. The team ensures that the products are manufactured in accordance with global standards and customer specifications at all times.

We also customize the packaging as per the requirements of the importing country. We also offer our products in private labels for any part and any language of the world.

Why Daiwik

Daiwik Pharmasphere was established in 2017, with the sole purpose of catering to the manufacturing of top-grade external preparations in the pharmaceutical industry. With our automatic production lines and highly trained personnel, we take pride in being one of the best quality producers of topicals.
